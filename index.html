Find Your Utility Bill Discount

Oregon's investor-owned utilities offer income-qualified bill discounts that can reduce your electric or gas bill by 10-95%.

Quick Facts
  • ✓ Self-attestation: No documentation required at application
  • ✓ Income-based: Programs serve households at or below 60% State Median Income
  • ✓ Multiple benefits: Bill discounts, arrearage relief, disconnection protections
  • ✓ All IOUs: PGE, Pacific Power, Idaho Power, NW Natural, Avista, Cascade Natural Gas
