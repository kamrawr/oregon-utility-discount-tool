CBO Coaching Notes

  • Lead with relief, not paperwork. "There's a monthly discount that can lower your bill. It's quick to apply and doesn't require uploading documents."
  • Build trust around PII. Walk through the form together on-screen. Let them skip anything that feels uncomfortable.
  • Emphasize stacking. These discounts work alongside LIHEAP, OEAP, and other assistance ‚Äî they don't replace it.
